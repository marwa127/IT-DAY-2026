What is Information Technology?

Information Technology (IT) refers to the use of computers, software, networks, and digital systems to store, process, and share information. IT helps businesses, schools, hospitals, and individuals work more efficiently. From sending emails to managing large databases, IT plays a vital role in the modern world.
